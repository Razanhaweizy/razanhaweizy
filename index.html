21 — Blackjack Assistant (YOLOv8 + DBSCAN)

Real-time blackjack assistant that ingests live video and overlays the optimal move from basic strategy. Retrained a YOLOv8 detector on playing-card data to localize ranks/suits, then separated player vs. dealer cards with DBSCAN clustering on card centroids (fallback to farthest-point heuristic). Rendered running totals and recommended action directly on the frame.

Highlights: live video I/O, object detection fine-tuning, strategy engine, clustering for spatial disambiguation, on-screen visualization.

Tech: Python, YOLOv8 (Ultralytics), OpenCV, NumPy, DBSCAN
